Nigerians hope President Muhammadu Buhari meeting with security personel on
Thursday will end with solutions to all the different national challenges they have
gone through in recent times.

Today, President Muhammadu Buhari lead the Security Council Meeting, special meeting with top people in government to torchlight security in Nigeria with vice-president Yemi Osinbajo also present.

President @MBuhari presided over a Security Council Meeting today. Vice President
@ProfOsinbajo, NSA, Ministers, Service and Intelligence Chiefs in attendance. #AsoVillaToday

country, security issues everywhere.
